About

This hub highlights local non-profits, services, events, and programs for residents. Built as an educational project by South Florence High School TSA Chapter.

Goals
Provide accurate, accessible information on community resources. Enable discovery by category, service type, neighborhood, and community.
Contact
For updates or corrections, use the Suggest a Resource form or email the chapter.
